Our Process

How We Make Pure Ice & Water

Our production process follows strict food safety standards to ensure every product is safe for consumption.

🚰

1. Source Water

We use water from a certified deep well or municipal supply source that passes initial quality testing.

🔩

2. Pre-Filtration

Water passes through sediment and carbon filters to remove particulates, odor, and chlorine.

⚗️

3. Reverse Osmosis

RO membrane removes dissolved solids, bacteria, and contaminants down to microscopic levels.

☀️

4. UV Sterilization

UV light kills remaining bacteria and viruses for water that's 99.9% pure and safe to drink.

❄️

5. Ice Manufacturing

Purified water is fed into our stainless-steel tube ice machine operating at food-safe temperatures.

📦

6. Packaging & QC

Ice and water are packaged in clean, sealed containers and undergo final quality checks before delivery.
